Coalition for Good is a network of individuals, networks, businesses and organizations who are taking a visible, active Anti-Racism + Anti-Othering position and are committed to this work.

We are dedicated to our Common Good.
We view the world as a community: humans, animals, and the planet itself. We connect and support all who are doing the transformative work of creating a more compassionate, inclusive, just, and equitable world for all. We are a membership organization connecting all who are guided and inspired by our shared caring values and ideals.

Ethos

Healing, transformation and commitment to The Community Good begins with being aware
With awareness we can expand and deepen our capacity to care
With care comes connection and relationship
With relationship comes the opportunity to see truth and build trust
With trust comes heartfelt compassion and empathy
With compassion and empathy come justice and healing
With justice and healing comes peace
With PEACE we can create meaningful, joyful sustainable lives, communities and societies
BUT, FIRST, WE MUST CARE

Unity in diversity

Mission

Facilitate

Facilitate cultural, systemic transformation toward our shared Anti-Racism + Anti-Othering vision via collaborative spaces, platforms and projects

Empower

Empower diverse, multi-sector organizations and communities in the transformative work of building caring, diverse, inclusive, just, equitable, resilient societies by providing educational materials, tools and programs to the public, business and organizations

Create

Create membership organizations and online platforms to connect individuals, organizations and businesses for the purpose of engaging in activities and projects that benefit the Community Good

Strengthen

Strengthen communities by supporting the many existing aligned grassroots, community-supportive projects and organizations; sponsor and incubate programs that encourage more diverse, inclusive, equitable community ownership; cultivate intersectoral collaborative connections and relationships

Collaborate

Create collaborative opportunities based upon the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals through a lens prioritizing racial justice + equity
